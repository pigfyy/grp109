Hello! My name's Ashton. I use he/they pronouns, I'm 21, and originally from Idaho. That's me in the photos, and they're just about the only ones of myself I'm happy with. I think it's much easier to relate to someone when you have some common ground, so let me lay out some of my interests for you:

Tech Stuff

I love anything and everything related to computers! I've first started programming in 5th grade, and never stopped. I've made everything from Unity and Unreal games to Python bots, Blender addons, simple AI, and mobile apps. I've also ran servers for multiple projects, both self-hosted and remote. Tech's acted as my consistent interest thoughout life, and a career in the field is my main ambition.
