Our intial goal

Description

We wanted to create a pizza restaurant website is an interactive restaurant menu website where users can browse dishes, customize their meals, and can explore special offers. It features a responsive design, user-friendly navigation, and a mock payment system for a realistic experience.

Purpose

To provide users with an engaging and convenient way to interact with a restaurant's menu online. This site focuses on enhancing user experience through interactivity, accessibility, and visual appeal.

The goals we accomplished

  • Interactive restaurant menu
  • Customizable meal
  • Explore special offers
  • user-friendly navigation
  • Mock payment system
  • Visual appeal

The goals we didn't get to

  • Responsive design

    A responsive design would ensure that our website works smoothly on all screens (phone, tablet, desktop). Everything we learnt this quarter was focused on creating sites for laptops. Throughout the project we focused on optimizing a laptop user's experience, and could expand on our project later by ensuring it works on all other screens.

  • Accessible design

    While we did learn about making designs accessible, our team chose not to prioritize this goal (aside from text readability). With the time given, we felt it was best to focus our energy on making the design as intercative and easy to navigate as possible.In the future, we could continue the project and test the site for accessibility.
